Shahrukh Khan

Shah Rukh Khan was born 2 November 1965, also known by the initialism SRK, is an Indian actor and film producer who works in Hindi films.He is Referred to in the media as the "Baadshah of Bollywood", "King of Bollywood" and "King Khan".

Early Life and Education

  • 1) Khan was born on 2 November 1965 into a Muslim family in New Delhi. He spent the first five years of his life in Mangalore, where his maternal grandfather, Iftikhar Ahmed, served as the chief engineer of the port in the 1960s.
  • 2) Khan grew up in the Rajendra Nagar neighbourhood of New Delhi. His father had several business ventures including a restaurant, and the family lived a middle-class life in rented apartments.
  • 3) Khan completed his schooling in St. Columba’s School and Bachelor's in Economics from Hans Raj College, University of Delhi. After that he does his Masters in Journalism and Mass Communication from Jamia Milia Islamia. Furthermore he got Honorary Doctorate from La Trobe University
  • 4) Right from the beginning, Shahrukh Khan took a keen interest in studies. He participated in the extra-curricular activities as much as he took part in academics. Braving all the hardships.

National Recognition

  • 1) Khan began his acting career from TV starring in his first role in Lekh Tandon’s Dil Dariya which started shooting in 1988 but there were some delays in the production that led to Fauji directed by Raj Kumar Kapoor as his first debut on TV.
  • 2) In 1993, SRK signed around four movies, including Dil Aashna Hai, Deewana, Chamatkar and Raju Ban Gya Gentleman.
  • 3) He won the Filmfare Best Debut Male Award for Deewana and was lauded for the physical energy, raw enthusiasm and a fresh breather that he brought to the silver screen!
  • 4) His most significant release of 1995 was Aditya Chopra's directorial debut, the romance "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge", in which he played a young Non-resident Indian (NRI) who falls in love with Kajol's character during a trip across Europe. Khan was initially reticent to portray the role of a lover, but this film is credited with establishing him as a "romantic hero".

Top 5 Highest-Grossing Movies

  • 1) Chennai Express was released in 2013 and directed by Rohit Shetty which is one of the top-grossing movies by Shahrukh Khan. The worldwide gross collection was ₹424.54 Crores.
  • 2) Happy New Year movie was directed by Farah Khan Kunder and was released in 2014. The worldwide gross collection was ₹383.1 Crores.
  • 3) Dilwale is another popular romantic film by Shahrukh khan with Kajol. This was released in 2015 and directed by Rohit Shetty. This film also features Varun Dhawan. The worldwide gross collection was ₹376.85 Crores.
  • 4) Raees is a thrilling movie by Shah Rukh Khan, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Mahira Khan and Mohammed Zeeshan Ayyub is the main cast of this movie. This was released in 2017 and directed by Rahul Dholakia. The worldwide gross collection was ₹281.44 Crores.
  • 5) Jab Tak Hai Jaan is a romantic film directed by Yash Chopra. Shah Rukh Khan, Katrina Kaif and Anushka Sharma are the main cast of this movie. The worldwide gross collection was ₹235.66 Crores.

Personal life

  • 1) King Khan married Gauri Chibber, a Punjabi Hindu, in a traditional Hindu wedding ceremony on 25 October 1991,
  • 2) After a six-year courtship. They have a son Aryan (born 1997) and a daughter Suhana (born 2000). In 2013, they became parents of a third child, Abraham, who was born through a surrogate mother.
  • 3) His father died of cancer in 1981, and his mother died in 1991 from complications of diabetes. After the death of their parents, his elder sister, Shahnaz Lalarukh fell into a depressed state and Khan took on the responsibility of caring for her.
  • 4) Shahrukh owns Red Chilli Entertainment which is a movie production and distribution company. He also owns a 55% stake in Indian Premier League’s Franchises Team Kolkata Knight Riders and a 26% stake in Kidzania Franchise.

Some Lesser Known Facts

  • 1) At the time of SRK's birth, he was named Abdul Rahman by his maternal grandmother; however, his father letter named him Shahrukh Khan.
  • 2) His family used to live in rental houses. Initially, they lived in a bungalow in Rajinder Nagar's F Block. Later, when Shahrukh Khan turned 15, they shifted to Gautam Nagar.
  • 3) During an interview, he told that his mother was a South Indian and belong to Andhra Pradesh, who later moved to Karnataka.
  • 4) Shahrukh Khan has an immense love for video games.
  • 5) He was awarded the "sword of honour" title at St. Columbia School, New Delhi, which was assigned to the best student of the school.
  • 6) He was just 15 years old when his father died due to cancer in 1981.
  • 7) In his youth, he used to mimic the legendary Bollywood actors like Dilip Kumar, Amitabh Bachchan, Mumtaz, etc.
